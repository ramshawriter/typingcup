Accented letters trip up a lot of people writing in Spanish, French, or German. Copy any accent from the symbols above, or learn the quick method for your device below.

## The fastest method per device

| Device | How |
| --- | --- |
| Mac | Hold the base letter (e.g. <kbd>e</kbd>) until accent options pop up |
| iPhone / Android | Long-press the base letter, slide to the accent |
| Windows | Use the accent key combos below, or <kbd>Win</kbd> + <kbd>.</kbd> |

## On Mac (the easy way)

Press and **hold** the letter you want to accent — hold <kbd>e</kbd> and a little menu of è é ê ë appears. Press the number shown under the accent you want. This works in nearly every Mac app.

There's also the classic combo: <kbd>Option</kbd> + <kbd>e</kbd> then <kbd>e</kbd> gives é; <kbd>Option</kbd> + <kbd>n</kbd> then <kbd>n</kbd> gives ñ; <kbd>Option</kbd> + <kbd>u</kbd> then <kbd>u</kbd> gives ü.

## On iPhone and Android

Long-press the base letter on the keyboard. Hold <kbd>a</kbd> for à á â ä, hold <kbd>n</kbd> for ñ, hold <kbd>u</kbd> for ü. Slide your finger to the one you want and let go.

## On Windows

Windows is the fiddly one. The reliable options:

- **Emoji & symbol picker:** press <kbd>Win</kbd> + <kbd>.</kbd>, then search the letter.
- **Alt codes:** hold <kbd>Alt</kbd> and type the code on the number pad — <kbd>0233</kbd> = é, <kbd>0225</kbd> = á, <kbd>0241</kbd> = ñ, <kbd>0252</kbd> = ü.
- **US-International keyboard:** switch layouts and the apostrophe/quote keys become "dead keys" for accents.

## Common accent alt codes (Windows)

| Letter | Alt code | Letter | Alt code |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| á | <kbd>0225</kbd> | é | <kbd>0233</kbd> |
| í | <kbd>0237</kbd> | ó | <kbd>0243</kbd> |
| ú | <kbd>0250</kbd> | ñ | <kbd>0241</kbd> |
| ü | <kbd>0252</kbd> | ç | <kbd>0231</kbd> |

## FAQ

### How do I type a capital accented letter?
Same methods — just hold <kbd>Shift</kbd> first, or long-press the capital letter on a phone. For alt codes, the capital versions have their own numbers (e.g. <kbd>0201</kbd> = É).
